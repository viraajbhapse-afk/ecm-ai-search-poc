# POL-GOV-007: Records Retention Policy
Version: 5.0 | Effective: 01-Jan-2026 | Owner: Corporate Governance

## Retention Periods
- Financial records and invoices: 10 years
- Contracts and agreements: 7 years after expiry
- Employee records: 6 years after separation
- Email correspondence (business): 3 years
- Project documentation: 5 years after project closure
- Audit reports: permanent

## Disposal
Records past retention must be disposed via the approved secure destruction process and a disposal certificate filed in the ECM system.
