# POL-HR-012: Travel and Expense Policy
Version: 4.3 | Effective: 01-Jun-2025 | Owner: Human Resources

## Per Diem (UAE-based staff)
- Domestic (within UAE): AED 250/day
- GCC travel: AED 400/day
- International: AED 600/day

## Approval Limits
- Trips under AED 5,000: line manager approval
- AED 5,000-20,000: department head
- Above AED 20,000: CFO approval required

## Claims
Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of travel completion with original receipts. Late claims require CFO exception approval.
